<h2>1. Common Technical Issues on XM</h2>
<ul>
<li>Login failures or authentication errors</li>
<li>Slow or delayed order execution</li>
<li>Platform freezing or crashing</li>
<li>Price feed or chart errors</li>
<li>Deposit or withdrawal system glitches</li>
</ul>

<h2>2. When Should You Report an Issue?</h2>
<ul>
<li>When the problem persists after restarting the platform</li>
<li>When multiple login attempts fail</li>
<li>When trades cannot be executed properly</li>
<li>When system errors affect account access</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Step-by-Step: How to Report Issues on XM</h2>
<ul>
<li>Go to XM official website</li>
<li>Open the “Support” or “Help Center” section</li>
<li>Select Live Chat, Email, or Contact Form</li>
<li>Describe your issue clearly</li>
<li>Submit screenshots if possible</li>
</ul>
