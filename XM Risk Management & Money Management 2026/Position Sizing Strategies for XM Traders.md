<h2>1. What is Position Sizing?</h2>
<p>Position sizing refers to the process of determining how much capital to allocate to a single trade based on risk tolerance and strategy.</p>

<h2>2. Why Position Sizing Matters</h2>
<ul>
<li>Controls risk exposure</li>
<li>Prevents large losses</li>
<li>Ensures long-term survival</li>
<li>Improves consistency</li>
</ul>

<table>
<tr><th>Method</th><th>Description</th></tr>
