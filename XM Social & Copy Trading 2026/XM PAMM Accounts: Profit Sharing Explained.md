<h2>1. What is an XM PAMM Account?</h2>
<p>A PAMM account pools funds from multiple investors into a single managed account operated by a professional trader (manager). The manager executes trades, and profits or losses are distributed proportionally among all investors.</p>

<ul>
<li>No need to trade manually</li>
<li>Access to professional fund management</li>
<li>Transparent performance tracking</li>
</ul>

<h2>2. How Profit Sharing Works</h2>
<p>Profit sharing in PAMM accounts follows a structured model:</p>

<table>
<tr><th>Component</th><th>Description</th></tr>
<tr><td>Total Investment</td><td>Combined funds from all investors</td></tr>
<tr><td>Manager Trading</td><td>Trades executed by professional trader</td></tr>
<tr><td>Profit Distribution</td><td>Shared based on investment percentage</td></tr>
<tr><td>Performance Fee</td><td>Manager receives a percentage of profits</td></tr>
</table>

<p><strong>Example:</strong> If you invest $1,000 in a $10,000 PAMM account, you own 10%. If the account gains 20%, your profit is $200 minus the manager’s fee.</p>
