<h2>1. What is Trend Following?</h2>
<p>Trend following is a strategy that aims to capture profits by trading in the direction of the market trend.</p>

<h2>2. What is Counter-Trend Trading?</h2>
<p>Counter-trend trading involves taking positions against the current trend, anticipating reversals or pullbacks.</p>
