<h2>1. What Are Moving Averages?</h2>
<p>Moving averages are indicators that smooth out price data over a specific period to identify the direction of a trend.</p>

<h2>2. Types of Moving Averages</h2>

<table>
<tr><th>Type</th><th>Description</th></tr>
<tr><td>Simple Moving Average (SMA)</td><td>Averages price over a fixed period</td></tr>
<tr><td>Exponential Moving Average (EMA)</td><td>Gives more weight to recent prices</td></tr>
</table>

<h2>3. How Moving Averages Work</h2>
<ul>
<li>Price above MA = Uptrend</li>
<li>Price below MA = Downtrend</li>
<li>Crossovers signal potential reversals</li>
</ul>
