<h2>1. What Are Trading Fees on XM?</h2>
<p>XM trading fees refer to the costs incurred when opening, holding, or closing trades. These include spreads, commissions, and swap fees.</p>

<h2>2. What Is a Spread?</h2>
<p>A spread is the difference between the buy (ask) and sell (bid) price of a trading instrument.</p>

<ul>
<li>Tight spread = lower trading cost</li>
<li>Wide spread = higher trading cost</li>
<li>Spreads vary by account type and market conditions</li>
</ul>

<h2>5. Swap Fees (Overnight Charges)</h2>
<p>Swap fees are charged when positions are held overnight.</p>

<ul>
<li>Based on interest rate differences</li>
<li>Can be positive or negative</li>
<li>Avoidable with intraday trading</li>
</ul>
