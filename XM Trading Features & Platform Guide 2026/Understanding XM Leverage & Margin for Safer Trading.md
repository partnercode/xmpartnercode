<h1>Understanding XM Leverage & Margin for Safer Trading</h1>

<p>Leverage and margin are two of the most important concepts in trading on XM. In 2026, understanding how they work is essential for managing risk and avoiding unnecessary losses. While leverage can increase profit potential, it also increases risk if not used properly.</p>

<div class="highlight">
<strong>Key Insight:</strong> Leverage is a double-edged sword—used correctly, it helps growth; used poorly, it can quickly wipe out your account.

<h2>1. What Is Leverage on XM?</h2>
<p>Leverage allows traders to control larger positions with a smaller amount of capital. For example, 1:100 leverage means you can control $10,000 with only $100.</p>

<h2>2. What Is Margin?</h2>
<p>Margin is the amount of money required to open and maintain a trading position. It acts as a security deposit for your trades.</p>

<ul>

</ul>

<h2>6. Risks of High Leverage</h2>
<ul>
<li>Amplified losses</li>
<li>Margin calls</li>

</ul>

<h2>7. How to Use Leverage Safely</h2>
<ul>
<li>Use low leverage for beginners</li>
<li>Always set Stop Loss</li>
<li>Risk only 1–2% per trade</li>
<li>Avoid overtrading</li>
</ul>
