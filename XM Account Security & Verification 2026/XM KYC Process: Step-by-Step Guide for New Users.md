<h2>1. What Is XM KYC?</h2>
<p>XM KYC is a verification process that confirms a trader’s identity and address using official documents before allowing full account usage.</p>

<h2>2. Why KYC Is Required</h2>
<ul>
<li>Prevents fraud and identity misuse</li>
<li>Ensures regulatory compliance</li>
<li>Protects trader funds and accounts</li>
<li>Enables withdrawals and deposits</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Documents Needed for KYC</h2>

<table>
<tr><th>Type</th><th>Accepted Documents</th></tr>
<tr><td>Identity Proof</td><td>Passport, National ID, Driver’s License</td></tr>
<tr><td>Address Proof</td><td>Utility bill, bank statement, tax document</td></tr>
</table>

<h2>4. Step-by-Step XM KYC Process</h2>
<ul>
<li>Log in to your XM account</li>
<li>Access the verification/KYC section</li>
<li>Upload identity document</li>
<li>Upload proof of address</li>
<li>Submit for review</li>
<li>Wait for XM confirmation</li>
</ul>

<h2>5. Tips for Successful KYC Verification</h2>
<ul>
<li>Use clear, high-resolution images</li>
<li>Ensure documents are valid and not expired</li>
<li>Match all personal details exactly</li>
<li>Avoid cropped or edited files</li>
</ul>

<h2>6. Common KYC Mistakes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Blurry document uploads</li>
<li>Name mismatch between account and documents</li>
<li>Expired identification documents</li>
<li>Incorrect file format submissions</li>
</ul>

<h2>7. How Long Does XM KYC Take?</h2>
<p>Most XM KYC verifications are completed within a few hours to 1–2 business days depending on document accuracy and system workload.</p>
