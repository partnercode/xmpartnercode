<h2>1. Why XM Account Security Matters</h2>
<p>Securing your XM account helps protect your funds, personal data, and trading activities from cyber threats and unauthorized access.</p>

<h2>2. Use a Strong Password</h2>
<ul>
<li>Create a unique password with letters, numbers, and symbols</li>
<li>Avoid using personal information like names or birthdays</li>
<li>Change your password regularly</li>
<li>Do not reuse passwords from other platforms</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)</h2>
<ul>
<li>Adds an extra layer of security</li>
<li>Requires a verification code when logging in</li>
<li>Reduces risk of unauthorized access</li>
<li>Highly recommended for all traders</li>
</ul>

<h2>4. Protect Yourself from Phishing Attacks</h2>
<ul>
<li>Do not click suspicious links in emails or messages</li>
<li>Always verify official XM website URLs</li>
<li>Avoid sharing login details with anyone</li>
<li>Report suspicious activity immediately</li>
</ul>

<h2>5. Secure Your Devices</h2>
<ul>
<li>Keep your software and browser updated</li>
<li>Use antivirus protection</li>
<li>Avoid logging in from public Wi-Fi</li>
<li>Log out after each session</li>
</ul>

<h2>6. Monitor Account Activity</h2>
<ul>
<li>Check login history regularly</li>
<li>Review open positions and transactions</li>
<li>Set alerts for account activity</li>
<li>Report unusual activity immediately</li>
</ul>

<h2>7. Safe Trading Habits</h2>
<ul>
<li>Avoid emotional trading decisions</li>
<li>Use stop-loss orders on every trade</li>
<li>Do not overleverage positions</li>
<li>Withdraw profits regularly</li>
</ul>

<h2>8. Common Security Mistakes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Using weak or repeated passwords</li>
<li>Ignoring 2FA setup</li>
<li>Clicking unknown links</li>
<li>Sharing account details with others</li>
</ul>
