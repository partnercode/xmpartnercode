<h2>1. What is P&amp;L in Trading?</h2>
<p>P&amp;L (Profit and Loss) represents the total gains or losses from your trades. It includes both realized profits (closed trades) and unrealized profits (open trades).</p>

<h2>2. Understanding Margin Levels</h2>

<table>
<tr><th>Term</th><th>Description</th></tr>
<tr><td>Balance</td><td>Total funds in account</td></tr>
<tr><td>Equity</td><td>Balance + unrealized P&amp;L</td></tr>
<tr><td>Used Margin</td><td>Funds locked for open trades</td></tr>
<tr><td>Free Margin</td><td>Available funds for new trades</td></tr>
<tr><td>Margin Level</td><td>Equity / Used Margin × 100%</td></tr>
</table>
